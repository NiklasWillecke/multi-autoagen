# Example inputs for the Group Movie Recommendation System
# ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
# Web UI: Paste the text under "Film preferences" for each person.
# API: JSON body for POST /api/recommend (see scenarios below).
# ---------------------------------------------------------------------------


## Scenario 1: Two friends – similar taste (easy consensus)
# Alice:
I like exciting movies with interesting characters, emotional moments, and a gripping plot. I especially enjoy thrillers, science fiction, and dramas with surprising twists. At least 7.0 on TMDB.

# Bob:
Entertaining movies with humor, adventure, and atmosphere — especially action, fantasy, and comedy. A good story matters more than special effects. No horror please.


## Scenario 2: Couple – different preferences (conflict)
# Lisa:
Romantic comedies or light dramas, preferably with lots of wit and charming characters. Feel-good movies for a relaxed evening. No violence, no horror.

# Tom:
Dark thrillers and crime films with twists, psychological tension, and a moody atmosphere. Movies like Inception or Se7en. Action is fine, but no cheesy romance.


## Scenario 3: Family night – parents + teenager
# Mom:
Family-friendly movies that still entertain adults. Warm, funny, not too long. Animation or adventure works great. No explicit horror.

# Dad:
Exciting action or science fiction with a strong story and compelling characters. Something nerdy or futuristic is a plus. At least 6.5 rating.

# Teen (Mia):
Cool coming-of-age films or fantasy with strong protagonists. Visually impressive, not too childish. Comedy or drama — definitely not boring family movies.


## Scenario 4: Film club – four people, mixed genres
# Anna:
European arthouse films, slow storytelling, great acting, philosophical themes. Prefers drama and mystery.

# Ben:
Blockbuster action with special effects, superheroes, fast pace. Popcorn cinema, nothing too serious.

# Clara:
Documentaries or inspiring biopics about real people. Emotional and thought-provoking.

# David:
Horror and mystery with supernatural elements, dark mood, unexpected twists. No romance.


## Scenario 5: Friend group – finding a compromise (5 people)
# Alex:
Sci-fi with philosophical questions, time travel, or dystopian worlds. Blade Runner vibes.

# Jordan:
Classic comedies from the 90s or 2000s, lots of slapstick and witty dialogue.

# Sam:
War films or historical dramas, authentic and intense. No fantasy.

# Riley:
Anime-inspired animation or fantasy adventures, colorful worlds, hero's journey.

# Casey:
True-crime thrillers and investigative crime films, realistic and suspenseful until the end.


## Scenario 6: Horror night vs. scaredy-cats (3 people)
# Chris:
Hardcore horror, jump scares, supernatural monsters — as scary as possible. The Conjuring style.

# Pat:
Light mystery thrillers without gore, more psychological tension. No splatter.

# Morgan:
No horror at all! Only comedy or feel-good animation. Horror is an absolute no-go.

# API examples (curl)
# ---------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Scenario 1 – minimal (2 users, default settings):
curl -X POST http://localhost:3333/api/recommend \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "users": [
      {
        "user_id": "u1",
        "user_name": "Alice",
        "raw_text": "I like exciting movies with interesting characters, emotional moments, and a gripping plot. I especially enjoy thrillers, science fiction, and dramas with surprising twists."
      },
      {
        "user_id": "u2",
        "user_name": "Bob",
        "raw_text": "Entertaining movies with humor, adventure, and atmosphere — especially action, fantasy, and comedy. No horror please."
      }
    ]
  }'

# Scenario 2 – with agent discussion (3 voting rounds, 2 chat turns):
curl -X POST http://localhost:3333/api/recommend \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "users": [
      {
        "user_id": "u1",
        "user_name": "Lisa",
        "raw_text": "Romantic comedies or light dramas, feel-good vibes, no horror."
      },
      {
        "user_id": "u2",
        "user_name": "Tom",
        "raw_text": "Dark thrillers and crime films with twists, psychological tension. No cheesy romance."
      }
    ],
    "max_rounds": 3,
    "enable_discussion": true,
    "discussion_turns": 2
  }'
